About

Young people getting a first tattoo may have no clue or have misconceptions about the tattooing process. This causes frustration between artists & clients.

The goal of this online interface is to take a first step in educating these people getting their first tatoo, so they can kickstart their tattooing process with the right mindset and realistic expectations.
